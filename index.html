About

With 15 years of experience in computational biology, I integrate metagenomics, metaproteomics, metabolomics, transcriptomics, and clinical data to uncover host–microbiome mechanisms in chronic diseases (rheumatoid arthritis, multiple sclerosis, colorectal cancer). I develop ML/AI models for treatment response prediction and disease stratification, and build reproducible bioinformatics tools such as GMHI, GMWI2, TaxiBGC, and BPGA. My focus is translational: converting complex datasets into actionable diagnostics, wellness monitoring, and targeted therapies.
